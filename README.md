# AI Solar Lead Qualification

## Overview

This project automates the lead qualification process for a solar company using AI.

The workflow collects customer information from a Tally form, analyzes the lead using Google Gemini AI, stores the data in Google Sheets, sends Slack notifications, and automatically sends emails through Gmail.

---

## Workflow

Tally Form
↓
Google Gemini AI
↓
Lead Qualification
↓
Google Sheets
↓
Slack Notification
↓
Gmail Notification
↓
Customer Auto Reply

---

- AI Lead Scoring

## Lead Qualification Logic

| Score | Status |
|-------|--------|
| 80-100 | 🔥 Hot |
| 50-79 | 🟡 Warm |
| 0-49 | ❄️ Cold |
